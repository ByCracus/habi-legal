Habi Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

Habi is a habit and workout tracking app. We collect and process the data needed to provide account access, sync your history across devices, and support optional social features.

Data We May Collect

  • Account data such as email address, username, friend code, and authentication identifiers.
  • App content such as habits, logs, routines, workouts, notes, exercises, break-day data, and settings.
  • Technical and support data required to keep the app functioning, such as sync metadata, notification scheduling data, and support communications.

How We Use Data

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To sync your history and preferences across devices when you sign in.
  • To provide reminders, workout tracking, social features, and related support.
  • To process account deletion or support requests.

Data Sharing

Habi does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers required to operate the app, such as Firebase authentication and database services. Social data is only shown when you actively use sharing features.

Retention and Deletion

Local-only data remains on your device until you remove it or uninstall the app. Cloud account data is retained while your account exists. If you request account deletion, associated account data should be deleted unless retention is required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
