Habi Account Deletion

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Delete Inside the App

If you are signed in, open Habi, go to your profile, and use the Delete account option. For security reasons, you may need to sign in again before the final account removal step succeeds.

Delete Outside the App

If you cannot access the app, contact support and request account deletion using the email address associated with your account.

Request deletion by email

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile information.
  • Synced habits, logs, workouts, notes, break-day data, and exercise items associated with your account.
  • Social references owned by your account and synced metadata required to operate the account.

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Some technical or security-related records may be retained temporarily when required for abuse prevention, security, or legal compliance.
